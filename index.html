Portfolio

BEAUTY HAVEN

Beauty Haven is a front-end e-commerce website specializing in beauty and personal care products. The site features a user-friendly interface with a soft pink color palette and offers a wide range of cosmetics, skincare, hair care, and fragrance items. Users can browse products by category, register and log in with ease, and enjoy a seamless shopping experience. The website is built using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript, ensuring a responsive and visually appealing design.
